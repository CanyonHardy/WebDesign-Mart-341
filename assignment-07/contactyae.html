Yae Publishing House

Owned and Operated by Yae Miko

Welcome to Yae Publushing House! We are a humble society of writers, reader, editiors, librarians, and book enjoyers. Yae Publishing House is a point of interest located in Inazuma City, Narukami Island, Inazuma. Referred to as a giant of the Inazuma publishing industry. We periodically hold a competition called "This Novel Is Amazing!" and are renowned throughout Teyvat. Boss and founder of the Yae publishin house is Yae Miko, the chief priestess of the Grand Narukami Shrine. Xingqiu and Albedo's collaboration on "The Legend of Sword" is not affiliated with the Yae Publishing House. However, as a result of the book's popularity in Inazuma and Fontaine, the Yae Publishing House has attempted to contract Albedo's artistic talents for their own novels.
